Snap-O

Android screenshots and screen recordings on macOS without the friction

Download for macOS Star / Fork on GitHub

Requires macOS 15+ and adb from Android Platform Tools.

  • Fast and clean

    Opens with an instant screenshot and starts or ends recordings without delay. Frame-by-frame previews make it easy to check your work right away.

  • Drag-and-drop everything

    Share screenshots and videos directly into GitHub, Slack, or any other tool, for easier collaboration.

  • Trigger from other apps

    Control Snap-O through Android Studio External Tools or your own hotkeys in other apps.
